# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 5, 2026**

Versal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Feedback Information
When you use the "Send Feedback" feature within the App, we collect:
- **Email Address:** Provided by you so we can respond to your feedback.
- **Message Content:** The feedback or bug report you provide.
- **Feedback Type:** The category of feedback you selected (e.g., General Feedback, Bug Report).

We do not collect any other personal information, such as your name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:
- Respond to your inquiries or support requests.
- Improve the App based on your feedback and bug reports.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use **Formspree** to process and deliver feedback submitted through the App. When you submit feedback, your email and message are sent to Formspree's servers. 

Formspree has its own privacy policy regarding how they handle data, which you can find at [https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/).

## 4. Data Retention

We retain feedback information for as long as necessary to address your feedback or improve the App. We do not use this information for marketing purposes.

## 5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the feedback form within the App.
